About

I'm Caio Everest, a computer scientist and a software engineer. I've worked on backend, frontend, and mobile as a developer but specialized on backend, focusing on the area of distributed transactions.

I am also an enthusiast of IoT and of integrating our everyday more with technology. I love building tools that help developers be more productive and systems that scale.
